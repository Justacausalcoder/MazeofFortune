The Maze of Fortune

In a world where a society is undergoing a terrible pandemic, the townspeople and even the president are in a crisis. The Golden Curse has affected many as they are encapsulated in pure gold, dying in the terrorizing process. The President has had enough of this debacle and wants someone to kill the Golden Skeleton, the suspected culprit of the chaos. They want you to be recruited as you have done good for the town before as a local community service member and salesperson. They tell you to venture into the Maze of Fortune to find the culprit. Will you take the job and Kill the Golden Skeleton, or venture into the deeper and darker truths and secrets of the Maze? The task is up to you in this maze roguelike.
